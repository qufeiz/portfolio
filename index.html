/ About

I care about the whole system — the part between the tools where real work leaks out.

I'm the engineer behind the studio — a full-stack and AI engineer who likes building products that actually run in production, with real users, real payments, and real consequences when something breaks. Recently that's meant a construction operating system used by contractors, and an agentic system that makes Federal Reserve data answerable in plain English.

Both were team efforts, and I think that's the point: the interesting engineering shows up where systems and people meet. I'm drawn to honest tests, data-layer security, and architectures that stay legible as they grow.

  • 01

    Product engineering

    Layered React + TypeScript frontends with business logic kept out of components — pages → context → services. Multi-tenant data models secured at the database, not just the UI.

  • 02

    AI / agentic systems

    Multi-agent RAG pipelines on LangGraph: hybrid retrieval (BM25 + vector) fused with reciprocal rank fusion, MCP-exposed tools, and guardrails treated as first-class, not an afterthought.

  • 03

    Ship & operate

    Continuous deploy gated by real journey tests that assert the actual outcome — the row persisted, the email sent — not a green status pill. Cloud-native on Supabase and AWS.
